## Introduction

Welcome to **EnvironmentGPT**, an AI-powered environmental knowledge assistant developed by the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) Punjab. This system helps you find accurate, relevant information about environmental topics using advanced artificial intelligence.

### What EnvironmentGPT Can Do

- **Answer Environmental Questions**: Get accurate answers about air quality, water resources, climate change, biodiversity, and more
- **Provide Source Citations**: Every response includes references to official documents and reports
- **Support Multiple Audiences**: Responses can be tailored for the general public, technical professionals, or policy makers
- **Track Conversations**: Your chat history is saved for easy reference

### Key Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| 💬 Natural Language Queries | Ask questions in plain English or Urdu |
| 📚 Source Citations | See where information comes from |
| 🎯 Audience Modes | Tailored responses for different users |
| 📊 Confidence Scores | Know how reliable the answer is |
| 📝 Conversation History | Access previous conversations |

## Using the Chat Interface

### Asking Questions

1. **Click on the input box** at the bottom of the screen
2. **Type your question** in English or Urdu
3. **Press Enter** or click the **Send button**
4. **Wait for the response** (usually takes 5-15 seconds)

### Example Questions

#### Air Quality
- "What are the current PM2.5 levels in Lahore?"
- "How does smog affect human health?"
- "What measures can reduce industrial emissions?"

#### Water Resources
- "What is the water quality status of River Ravi?"
- "Explain the groundwater contamination issues in Punjab"
- "What are the industrial effluent standards?"

#### Climate Change
- "How is climate change affecting Punjab's agriculture?"
- "What are the main sources of greenhouse gases in Pakistan?"
- "Explain the National Climate Change Policy"

#### Biodiversity
- "What endangered species are found in Punjab?"
- "Describe the wetlands of Pakistan"
- "What conservation efforts exist for local wildlife?"

### Understanding Response Quality

Each response includes a **confidence indicator**:

| Indicator | Meaning |
|-----------|---------|
| 🟢 High Confidence | Strong source support, reliable answer |
| 🟡 Medium Confidence | Partial sources, verify important details |
| 🔴 Low Confidence | Limited sources, additional research recommended |

1. Click the **📋 Sources** button on any response
2. A panel will open showing all referenced documents

## Managing Conversations

### Starting a New Conversation

1. Click the **💬 New Chat** button
2. A fresh conversation session begins
3. Previous conversations are saved in the sidebar

### Accessing Previous Conversations

1. Click the **sidebar menu** (☰) on the left
2. Your conversation history appears
3. Click any conversation to continue it

### Conversation Tips

- **Stay on topic**: Related questions build better context
- **Be specific**: Detailed questions get better answers
- **Use follow-up questions**: The system remembers context within a conversation

## Providing Feedback

### Rating Responses

After each response, you can provide feedback:

- **👍 Helpful**: The answer was accurate and useful
- **👎 Not Helpful**: The answer was inaccurate or unhelpful

### Adding Comments

1. Click the feedback button
2. Select the rating
3. Optionally add a comment explaining your rating
4. Submit your feedback

### Why Feedback Matters

Your feedback helps improve the system by:
- Identifying inaccurate responses
- Improving document retrieval
- Training better response models
- Prioritizing document updates

## Tips for Better Results

### Be Specific

❌ **Vague**: "Tell me about pollution"

✅ **Specific**: "What are the main sources of air pollution in Lahore and what are their health impacts?"

### Use Technical Terms When Appropriate

❌ **Too Simple**: "Why is the air bad?"

✅ **With Terminology**: "What are the primary sources of PM2.5 in urban Punjab and how do they compare to WHO guidelines?"

### Specify Your Audience

The system can tailor responses for:
- **General Public**: Simple explanations, everyday language
- **Technical**: Detailed data, scientific terminology
- **Policy Maker**: Executive summaries, policy implications

### Ask Follow-Up Questions

Build on previous responses:
1. "Can you provide more details about [specific aspect]?"
2. "How does this compare to previous years?"
3. "What are the legal requirements for this?"

### Request Specific Formats

- "List the top 5 sources..."
- "Create a comparison table of..."
- "Summarize the key points in bullet form"

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### "Response taking too long"

- **Cause**: Complex query or high server load
- **Solution**: Wait a few more seconds or simplify your question

#### "No relevant sources found"

- **Cause**: Question topic not in knowledge base
- **Solution**: Rephrase using different terms or contact EPA for document requests

#### "Error processing request"

- **Cause**: Temporary system issue
- **Solution**: Refresh the page and try again

#### "Session expired"

- **Cause**: Long period of inactivity
- **Solution**: Refresh the page to start a new session
